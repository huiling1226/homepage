I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU), advised by Prof. Jianliang Xu. Prior to this, I received my M.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology (advised by Prof. Yafei Li) and my B.Eng. in Software Engineering from Zhengzhou University in 2024 and 2021, respectively, where I was advised by Prof. Yafei Li during my master's studies.

My research focuses on vector database and spatial data management. I am particularly interested in Vector Databases & ANNS.

My work has been published at database, AI, and data mining venues including SIGMOD, PVLDB, ICDE, AAAI, and IEEE TMC. I am always open to insightful discussions and research collaborations in the above areas.
